📞 Master Phone Script

Read this word-for-word until it feels natural. Keep each call under 60 seconds.

When someone answers
"Hi, my name is Rishi [Last Name]. I'm a rising senior in high school and I'm planning to apply to BS/MD pre-medical programs this fall. I'm looking to gain some clinical experience this summer through physician shadowing or volunteering. Is there someone I could speak with about whether your practice might be open to that?"
When connected to the office manager or physician
"Hi, I'm Rishi. I'm a high school senior applying to combined BS/MD medical programs, and I have two years of biomedical coursework from my school's health sciences academy. I'm very motivated and genuinely passionate about medicine. I was hoping to shadow a physician for a few hours a week this summer — even 4–6 hours weekly would be incredibly valuable for me. I'd be respectful of your schedule and happy to help the team in any way. Would that be something you might consider?"
If they say YES
"Thank you so much — I really appreciate it. Could I get your email so I can send a brief note with my background? I want to make it as easy as possible for you."
→ Send follow-up email the same day. Mark this card as "Yes" below.
If they say NO or are unsure
"I completely understand. Would it be okay if I sent a short email with my background, just in case anything opens up? I'm very flexible on timing."
→ Always leave the door open. A no today can become a yes in 2 weeks.
